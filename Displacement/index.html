Displacement tells the story of a home which has been ravaged by the war in Ukraine. During the experience, players will be able to explore an apartment in three different points in time where they will uncover the story of what has happened and what events unfolded inside the home. Through immsersive sounds and an eary atmosphere, Displacement will guide you through what it is like to live in a war torn world and show you just how unliveable these areas are.
